Food that feels like mutual aid (not paperwork)

Food Not Bombs

Free meal shares run by volunteers (many cities).

foodnotbombs.net

Community fridges

Neighborhood fridges stocked by locals. Search a map, then also check Instagram/FB in your city.

freedge.org (map)

Little Free Pantries

24/7 mini pantries (food + hygiene) run by neighbors.

Find a pantry (map) littlefreepantry.org

Lasagna Love

Neighbor-to-neighbor home-cooked meal deliveries (US + others).

Request a meal lasagnalove.org

Healthcare without insurance (low-cost and real options)

Community Health Centers (HRSA / FQHC)

Sliding-scale clinics in every state; primary care + often dental/mental health. Great default starting point.

Find a Health Center HRSA: Get health care

Free & Charitable Clinics (NAFC)

Volunteer/nonprofit clinics + charitable pharmacies. Search by ZIP.

Find a clinic Find clinics + pharmacies

Remote Area Medical (RAM) pop-up clinics

Free mobile clinics (medical/dental/vision) that rotate by location; check schedule.

ramusa.org Clinic schedule

Telehealth (often cheaper than urgent care)

  • Doctor On Demand — 24/7 virtual urgent care + mental health options.
    doctorondemand.com Urgent care info
  • Dr. B — low-cost online consults + “No-Cost Care” for people who qualify.
    drb.ai No-Cost Care info

Meds + supplies

NeedyMeds

Prescription assistance programs, discounts, and listings for clinics/dental care.

needymeds.org

Search terms on any local directory: medical equipment, diapers, wound care, incontinence supplies, first aid, naloxone.

Harm reduction (often the most “mutual aid” health infrastructure)

NEXT Distro (mail-based supplies + naloxone)

Mail-based harm reduction service (including naloxone options) and support resources.

nextdistro.org Naloxone page Get supplies

Reproductive care / abortion support

National Network of Abortion Funds

Find local funds that can help with costs, logistics, and support.

abortionfunds.org Find a fund

Peer support / mental health outside the “carceral” pipeline

Fireweed Collective

Healing Justice / Disability Justice mental health resources and mutual aid.

fireweedcollective.org

Wildflower Alliance

Peer support, harm reduction, advocacy, trainings.

wildfloweralliance.org Peer support line

Vet care without insurance

Pet Help Finder

Search financially-friendly vet care, pet food/supplies, temporary housing, vouchers, etc.

pethelpfinder.org

Emergency / urgent help

  • RedRover Relief Urgent Care Grants — helps fill a small gap for life-threatening situations.
    Urgent Care Grants

    Note Grants aren’t guaranteed; still seek care immediately if your pet is at risk.

Non-emergency assistance

  • The Pet Fund — financial assistance for veterinary care (often non-urgent; may have wait times).
    thepetfund.com Financial assistance info

Directories (useful, but not the whole strategy)

These can help you find local stuff fast, but mutual aid often lives on Instagram, community fridges, and neighborhood networks.

  • 211 (routing to local help)
    211.org
  • findhelp (ZIP search for programs)
    findhelp.org

If you’re overwhelmed

Pick one: (1) Find a community fridge or Little Free Pantry nearby. (2) Find a HRSA health center (sliding scale). (3) If you need same-day care, use Doctor On Demand or Dr. B. (4) For pets, start with Pet Help Finder.
